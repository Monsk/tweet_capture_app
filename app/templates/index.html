Brexit Twitter Analysis

About

The purpose of this project is to chronicle the process of Brexit through an analysis of the discourse on Twitter. Every hour 100 tweets referencing Brexit are saved to a database, creating a dataset that is currently around 200 000 tweets in size and growing by 2400 every day. This will continue until the process of Brexit is complete and the UK has left the European Union.

My intention is to keep building out the functionality of the site to enable you to explore the dataset through visualisations. I will be writing occasional blog posts on my findings on my site.
